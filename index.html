The rural-to-urban continuum in the Portuguese periphery

Pedro P. Palazzo

Azores Traditional Architecture Summer School 2022

American transect happens along the main axis.
European towns are different: there is a clear urban/rural boundary on each of the main roads. Does this mean they have no transect, as some have argued? I will argue the transect in colonial Portuguese towns actually happens across the major axes, in the depth of the blocks.
